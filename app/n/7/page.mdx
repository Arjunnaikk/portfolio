# gibmoni - milestone-based crowdfunding on solana

<br />

gibmoni is a crowdfunding platform built to fix the trust gap in on-chain fundraising: funds are locked in a central vault and released to the creator only in stages, as milestones are approved, instead of handing over the full amount up front. contributors get a real say through weighted community voting rather than just hoping the team delivers.

each milestone goes through a voting period where funder voting power scales with both contribution size and on-chain reputation, and approval is checked against a quorum of 30% of funders and 30% of capital before funds are released. helium's tuktuk automates the approval/rejection cranking, rejected milestones can be retried up to three times, and if a project ultimately fails, contributors can pull a proportional refund from whatever's left in the vault.

## tech stack

- **blockchain:** solana, anchor smart contracts
- **automation:** helium tuktuk (crank-turner)
- **frontend:** next.js
